Zones of Regulation

Just like traffic lights, our feelings have different zones. Click each zone to learn more!

🔵 Blue Zone
Slow & Low Energy
Sad, Tired, Sick, Bored

Tools to Try:

  • Take a break
  • Ask for a hug
  • Drink water
  • Stretch your body
🟢 Green Zone
Good to Go!
Happy, Calm, Focused, Proud

Keep it up!

  • Keep doing what you're doing
  • Help a friend
  • Learn new things
  • Be creative
🟡 Yellow Zone
Caution! Losing Control
Frustrated, Worried, Excited, Silly

Tools to Try:

  • Belly breaths
  • Take a walk
  • Push against a wall
  • Use positive self-talk
🔴 Red Zone
Stop! Big Feelings!
Angry, Terrified, Panicked, Elated

Tools to Try:

  • Count to 10
  • Take deep breaths
  • Ask for help
  • Take a time-out
